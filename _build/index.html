Key Equations¶

  1. Position data to wind
\[\begin{split}\begin{aligned} \mathrm{Speed} &= A \sqrt{\tan{[\arccos{(\cos{(\mathrm{PITCH})} - \cos{(\mathrm{ROLL})})}]}} + B \\ \\ \mathrm{Direction} &= \arctan{\Bigg[\frac{-\cos{(\mathrm{YAW})}\sin{(\mathrm{ROLL})}}{\sin{(\mathrm{YAW})}\sin{(\mathrm{ROLL})} + \sin{(\mathrm{PITCH})}\cos{(\mathrm{YAW})}\cos{(\mathrm{ROLL})}} \Bigg]} \end{aligned}\end{split}\]
  1. Thermistor resistance to temperature
\[\begin{aligned} T &= \frac{1}{A + B (log{R}) + C (log{R})^3} \end{aligned}\]
